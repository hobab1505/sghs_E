The Impact of News Framing
뉴스 프레이밍이 여론에 미치는 영향 · 빈칸 추론

Many news media studies use experimental designs to see whether citizens' opinions are influenced by different news content.

For example, in one study, researchers created newspaper stories to frame the death penalty in different ways.

One story constructed the death penalty as an affront to moral values (the morality frame), whereas another story emphasized that the policy was fundamentally flawed because innocent people might be executed (the innocence frame).

Some subjects read the story with the morality frame, and others read the one with the innocence frame.

Subjects then completed a questionnaire that asked them to list the important factors they considered when determining their opinion on the death penalty.

It was found that subjects exposed to the innocence frame were more likely to mention innocence-related considerations as important factors in determining their attitudes toward the death penalty than subjects presented with the morality frame.

Thus,                               .

빈칸 추론
① the death penalty was often framed negatively in news media
② the media's frame reflected intention, not the reality of the event
③ the subjects' attitudes towards the news were unrelated to their morality
④ the readers' belief system had a significant impact on their choice of news
⑤ the news frames shaped the ingredients of the subjects' death penalty attitudes
정답 및 일타강사 해설
정답: ⑤ the news frames shaped the ingredients of the subjects' death penalty attitudes
💡 일타강사 팁: 뉴스 미디어가 동일한 사건(사형 제도)에 대해 '도덕성(morality)'과 '무고함(innocence)'이라는 서로 다른 프레임(구조화된 틀)을 제공했을 때, 이를 읽은 실험 참가자들의 의견이 어떻게 달라지는지 묻는 지문입니다.

무고함 프레임의 기사를 읽은 사람은 자신의 태도를 결정할 때 무고함과 관련된 요소들을 더 많이 언급했습니다. 즉, 뉴스 기사가 어떤 프레임으로 쓰였느냐에 따라 독자의 생각과 태도를 구성하는 내용물(ingredients)이 달라진다는 것이죠.

따라서 결론을 나타내는 빈칸에는 '뉴스 프레임이 대상자들의 사형제도에 대한 태도를 구성하는 요소를 형성했다'는 ⑤번이 가장 적절합니다.

The impact of news framing on public opinion.
(뉴스 프레이밍이 여론에 미치는 영향)
StepLogicMy Summary
① Introduction Many media studies use experiments to examine whether different news content influences public opinion. 많은 미디어 연구들은 다양한 뉴스 콘텐츠가 여론에 미치는 영향을 실험을 통해 확인한다.

③ Dev 2 (Procedure) Participants read one of the framed stories and then listed the key factors shaping their attitudes. 참가자들은 기사를 읽고 자신의 태도를 결정한 주요 요소를 나열했다.

The Limit of Knowledge
미래 예측에 있어서 지식의 한계 · 빈칸 추론

The knowledge problem is most evident / when we discuss our ability to                               .

Most people would say that they "know" the Sun will rise tomorrow. However, can we call this a certainty?

It seems very likely, but it has also been predicted that the time will come when the Sun runs out of fuel, swells massively, and consumes the Earth.

We don't know if this prediction is true, but it is consistent with our best understanding and we cannot rule it out.

It is also possible that the Earth will explode due to some internal process with its molten core, which we had not anticipated.

A massive comet that our telescopes have not observed may crash into the Earth and destroy the planet.

These examples seem a bit extreme, but consider the 230,000 people who died in the tsunami in Sumatra in 2004, which resulted from an undersea megathrust earthquake that had not been anticipated.

The most reasonable prediction on that day was that it would be an average day, like so many days before it, not that a massive wave was going to destroy many thousands of lives; tragically, such was the case.

빈칸 추론
① reach consensus when perspectives clash
② predict that which has not yet been observed
③ account for biases in the information we gather
④ identify patterns in data that require deep insight
⑤ solve problems that require integrating knowledge
정답 및 일타강사 해설
정답: ② predict that which has not yet been observed
💡 일타강사 팁: 우리가 가진 지식으로 완벽하게 미래를 예측하는 것이 불가능하다는 것을 역설하는 지문입니다.

우리는 내일 아침 해가 뜬다는 사실을 '안다(know)'고 생각하지만, 아주 먼 미래에 태양이 폭발하거나 예상치 못한 혜성이 지구에 충돌하는 변수는 완벽히 배제할 수 없습니다. 2004년 수마트라 쓰나미도 수십만 명이 죽을 것이라곤 아무도 예측하지 못했죠.

결국, 우리가 겪는 '지식의 문제'란 아직 관찰되지 않은 변수들 때문에 완벽한 예측이 불가능하다는 데 있습니다. 따라서 빈칸에 들어갈 말은 '우리가 아직 관찰되지 않은 것을 예측하는 능력에 대해 논의할 때' 지식의 한계가 가장 뚜렷하다는 ②번이 정답입니다.

(미래 예측에 있어서 지식의 한계)

The Importance of True Acceptance
어려운 상황에 대한 진정한 수용의 중요성 · 빈칸 추론

Accepting or being at peace with things isn't easy. It takes time, patience and a fair dose of courage.

True acceptance isn't about trying to make something go away, as in, 'If I accept this then it will go away'.

That's a good way to get dispirited when the problem, the thing, doesn't go away.

Using acceptance to make something go away is actually non-acceptance masquerading as acceptance in order to get rid of that towards which you have a negative attitude.

Rather than getting dispirited we would be better to notice what happens when we fight with a situation or experience.

It escalates! We could then wonder what would happen if we stopped fighting with it.

Through true acceptance, the impact of such experiences starts to diminish and in their own time these experiences impact us less often.

It's as if we've been visited by a stray dog that keeps coming back because we keep feeding it, but soon it loses interest and goes away when it is not being fed.

                              .

빈칸 추론
① Acceptance works only if it makes the problem go away.
② Focusing on problems causes them to persist in our lives.
③ Seeking attention is a way for individuals to be comfortable.
④ We perform better when we receive recognition for our effort.
⑤ Pursuing popularity keeps people coming back into social circles.
정답 및 일타강사 해설
정답: ② Focusing on problems causes them to persist in our lives.
💡 일타강사 팁: 힘든 상황이나 문제를 다루는 '진정한 수용(True acceptance)'에 대한 지문입니다.

문제를 없애버리기 위해 억지로 수용하는 척하는 것은 가짜 수용(non-acceptance)이며, 우리가 상황과 싸우려 할수록 오히려 문제는 더 악화(escalates)된다고 설명합니다. 이를 먹이를 주면 계속 찾아오는 **'떠돌이 개(stray dog)'**에 비유했습니다.

개에게 주는 '먹이'는 바로 우리가 문제에 쏟는 '관심(Attention)'입니다. 먹이(관심)를 주지 않고 진정으로 내버려 둘 때(수용할 때) 문제는 스스로 사라지게 되죠. 따라서 빈칸에 들어갈 결론은 **'문제에 집중하는 것(관심을 주는 것)이 우리 삶에 문제를 지속하게 만든다'**는 ②번이 정답입니다.

03 Topic & 04 Logical Framework
The importance of true acceptance: Focusing on problems causes them to persist in our lives.
(진정한 수용의 중요성: 문제에 집중하는 것은 오히려 문제를 지속하게 만든다.)
StepLogicMy Summary
① Introduction Accepting situations and maintaining inner peace is challenging, requiring time, patience, and courage. 상황을 받아들이고 내면의 평화를 유지하는 것은 도전적이며, 시간과 인내, 용기가 필요하다.
② Development True acceptance does not mean trying to make problems disappear; instead, it involves allowing experiences without resisting or attempting to control them. 진정한 수용이란 문제를 억지로 사라지게 하려는 것이 아니다. 오히려 통제하거나 저항하지 않고 경험을 허용하는 것이다.
③ Conclusion When we genuinely accept situations, their negative impact diminishes over time, allowing us to respond with greater calm and less emotional escalation. 진정으로 수용할 때 그것의 부정적 영향력은 점차 줄어들며, 감정적 악화 없이 침착하게 반응할 수 있게 된다 (관심을 끄면 문제는 떠난다).
