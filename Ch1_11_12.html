01 문항
어휘 적절성 (Q1 + Q2)
Q1. 밑줄 친 ①~⑤ 중 문맥상 낱말의 쓰임이 적절하지 않은 것은?
① adapt
② individuals
③ acquire
④ learning
⑤ disperse → accumulate
정답 & 해설
Q1 정답: ⑤ disperse → accumulate
오답 이유: "This psychology allows human populations to disperse pools of adaptive information that greatly exceed the inventive capacities of individuals."

disperse = "분산시키다, 흩뜨리다" → 문맥상 인간이 집단적으로 개인 능력을 초과하는 적응 정보 풀(pools)을 모아서 유지한다는 내용이어야 함.

accumulate = "축적하다, 모으다" → 개인의 창의적 능력을 훨씬 초과하는 적응 정보를 축적할 수 있게 한다 = 문맥에 적합.

나머지 확인:
① adapt(적응하다): "helps us adapt to a wide range of environments" ✓
② individuals(개인들): "not smart enough as individuals to solve" ✓
③ acquire(얻다): "because we acquire information from others" ✓
④ learning(학습하다): "much better at learning from others" ✓

Market Efficiency & Social/Environmental Values
시장 효율성과 사회·환경 가치의 충돌
문맥상 낱말의 쓰임이 적절하지 않은 것 고르기
본문 — 원문

Reformist approaches assume that markets could operate in more equitable공정한 and environmentally reasonable ways, and that a market is the most ① efficient option available.

Yet the logic of market efficiencies generally rotates on purely ② monetary calculations, effectively reasoning in a hall of mirrors and marginalising소외시키다 real social and environmental values.

Familiar examples are conventional food retailers, such as supermarkets, which purchase fruit and vegetables as cheaply as possible.

③ Inexpensive produce might be grown using soil- and water-damaging fertilisers, by poorly paid illegal migrant workers, and transported long distances using more cheap labour and environmentally damaging practices.

However, organic farmers can only follow ecologically sound practices and make sure they pay their workers fair wages if the food costs more, meaning supply for a(n) ④ elite demand.

By its very nature, one never manages to escape the conundrums난제 that production for trade entails.

Real social and environmental values involved in caring for people and Earth highlight the inequities and ⑤ preservation of market-oriented activities.

Q1. 밑줄 친 ①~⑤ 중 문맥상 낱말의 쓰임이 적절하지 않은 것은?

② monetary
③ Inexpensive
④ elite

정답 & 해설
Q1 정답: ⑤ preservation → distortion
오답 이유: "Real social and environmental values ... highlight the inequities and preservation of market-oriented activities."

preservation = "보존, 유지" → 사회적·환경적 가치가 시장 지향적 활동의 불공평성과 보존을 부각시킨다는 것은 문맥에 맞지 않음.

distortion = "왜곡" (또는 neglect = "무시", limitation = "한계") → 진정한 사회적·환경적 가치는 시장 활동의 불공평성과 왜곡을 드러낸다 = 문맥 적합.

나머지 확인:
① efficient: 개혁주의적 접근의 전제로 "시장이 가장 효율적인 선택지" ✓
② monetary: "순수하게 금전적 계산에만 의존한다" ✓
③ Inexpensive: "저렴한 농산물이 환경 파괴적 방식으로 재배될 수 있다" ✓
④ elite: "음식 비용이 더 들면 소수 엘리트 수요만 충족한다" ✓

Situational Ethics & Competitive Behavior
상황 윤리와 경쟁적 행동
문법상 틀린 표현 고르기
본문 — 원문

Situational ethics상황 윤리 ① is an ethical theory that takes into account the context of a situation or an act when judging whether it is ethical.

Supporters of this theory willingly permit casting aside absolute moral standards.

In the absence of a universal standard or law, ② what matters is the outcome or consequences; so, the end justifies the means.

Possibly the following contrasting realities can help illustrate the application of situational ethics.

In a pickup game of basketball played among friends, everyone is expected to call his or her own fouls or acknowledge ③ knocking the ball out-of-bounds.

Caring about one's friends and maybe getting to keep playing with the group ④ lead to these actions.

But, once an organized game is played with officials, most athletes will not admit to the same fouls or violations as the end goal of winning is more important than expressing concern for competitors.

Situational ethics has been ⑤ extended by many athletes and coaches to mean trying to get away with as many actions on the field or court as possible to gain competitive advantages.

01 문항
어법 정확성 (Q1 + Q2)
Q1. 밑줄 친 ①~⑤ 중 문법상 틀린 것은?
① is
② what
③ knocking
④ lead → leads
⑤ extended
정답 & 해설
Q1 정답: ④ lead → leads
오답 이유: "Caring about one's friends and maybe getting to keep playing with the group lead to these actions."

주어 분석: "Caring about one's friends and maybe getting to keep playing with the group" → and로 연결된 두 동명사구가 주어.

핵심: 동명사구 2개가 and로 연결되더라도, 하나의 통일된 개념(친구를 아끼고 계속 함께 플레이하고 싶은 마음)으로 볼 때 단수 취급. 또는 문장 전체가 "이러한 행동들로 이어지는 것은 바로 ~이다"의 단수 주어 맥락 → leads(3인칭 단수).

나머지 확인:
① is: "Situational ethics is ~" — 단수 주어 → 단수 동사 ✓
② what: "what matters is ~" — 관계대명사 what이 이끄는 명사절이 주어 ✓
③ knocking: "acknowledge knocking ~" — acknowledge + 동명사(목적어) ✓
⑤ extended: "has been extended by ~" — 수동태 현재완료 ✓

Moral Outrage & Cooperative Society
도덕적 분노와 협동 사회 유지
문법상 틀린 표현 고르기
본문 — 원문

Moral outrage is the psychological tool that motivates people to punish wrongdoers잘못을 저지른 사람, even at cost or risk to themselves.

It is a "commitment device," something ① that commits people to punishment, even though trying to punish someone can be dangerous.

When we witness unjust harm, whether someone ② committing interpersonal violence or obvious cheating, we have an embodied physical reaction.

We get angry, our blood pressure spikes, our heart beats faster; we thirst for retribution징벌, 응징.

Outrage leads us to ignore the irrationality of risking our own safety to punish someone.

In any single situation, there is ③ rarely an immediate personal benefit for meting out내리다 punishment, and often there is real risk, because the person you are serving justice against could lash out at공격하다 you or get ④ revenged in the future.

But the powerful feeling of outrage leads us to momentarily forget this unfavorable evaluation and get ⑤ involved in a situation we would be better off avoiding, and science reveals how this feeling and forgetting is essential for maintaining a cooperative society.

* retribution: 징벌, 응징   ** mete out: (벌 등을) 내리다   *** lash out at: ~을 공격[비난]하다

정답 & 해설
Q1 정답: ④ revenged → revenge
오답 이유: "...could lash out at you or get revenged in the future."

구조 분석: "get + 과거분사" 구문 → get revenged = "복수당하다"처럼 보이지만,
revenge는 자동사·타동사 둘 다 가능하나 "get revenge (on)" = "복수하다"가 관용 표현.
문맥상 "상대방이 당신에게 복수할 수 있다"는 의미 → get revenge (on you) = 올바른 표현.

핵심 구별:
• get revenged → 비표준 표현
• get revenge → "복수하다" (관용 표현) ✓
• be revenged (on) → "복수하다" (격식체, 드문 표현)

나머지 확인:
① that: "something that commits people to punishment" → that은 something을 선행사로 받는 관계대명사 ✓
② committing: "whether someone committing interpersonal violence" → whether절 안의 분사구문 (= is committing) ✓
③ rarely: "there is rarely an immediate personal benefit" → 부사, 동사 수식 ✓
⑤ involved: "get involved in a situation" → get + 과거분사 (수동 의미) ✓
